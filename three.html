Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of exterior cleaning services including window cleaning, gutter cleaning, and siding washing. Our team uses eco-friendly products and advanced equipment to ensure your property looks its best. We tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each client.

How do I schedule a service?

You can schedule a service by filling out our online quote form or by calling us directly at 647-972-5326. Once you contact us, our team will work with you to find a convenient time, discuss your needs, and provide a detailed estimate.

What areas do you serve?

Our services cover the local communities in Guelph, Cambridge, and surrounding areas. If you're unsure whether your area is included, please give us a call, and we'll be happy to help.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We stand behind the quality of our work and offer guarantees on our services. If you're not completely satisfied, please reach out to us so we can resolve any issues promptly.

How should I prepare my property for cleaning?

We recommend clearing any obstacles from the areas to be cleaned. For window cleaning, remove any delicate items near the windows. For gutters and siding, ensure there are no loose objects that could be affected during the cleaning process. Our team will also provide specific preparation instructions when you schedule a service.

What makes your service different?

At Warrior Wash, we focus on quality, reliability, and customer care. Our team is locally owned and operated, which means we understand the unique needs of our community. We invest in state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products to ensure both efficiency and environmental responsibility.

How long does a typical cleaning service take?

The duration of our cleaning services varies by project size and specific needs. Typically, window cleaning for an average home takes around 1-2 hours, while siding cleaning might require 2-3 hours. We always provide a detailed estimate and time frame when scheduling your service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, major credit cards, and electronic payments. This flexibility ensures that our customers can choose the most convenient option for them.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to accommodate other customers and adjust our schedule accordingly.

Are your products environmentally friendly?

Yes, we are committed to sustainability. All our cleaning products are eco-friendly, ensuring a safe cleaning process for your family and the environment without compromising on quality.

How often should I schedule cleaning services?

The frequency depends on your property's needs and environmental factors. Many customers benefit from scheduling cleaning services every 3-6 months, but we can work with you to develop a tailored maintenance schedule.
